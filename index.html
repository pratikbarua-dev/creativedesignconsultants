Our Services

At Innovative Decor, we are committed to revolutionizing interior spaces with expertly crafted designs. Specializing in charcoal louvers, charcoal boards, HPL laminates, acrylic sheets, MDF acrylic boards, and Gorjon Ply HPL and acrylic boards, we pledge to deliver superior quality and creativity in every project.

Interior Design and Decoration

Creative and functional design solutions for homes, offices, restaurants, and showrooms.

Architectural and Interior Consultancy

Expert guidance on space planning, furniture selection, color schemes, and lighting design.

3D Exterior and Interior Design

Detailed 3D models to help clients visualize proposed designs for interiors and exteriors.

Decorative Materials

3D boards, HPL, acrylic boards, painting services, wallpaper, and artificial glass to enhance aesthetics.
